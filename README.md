# Diwali Sales Data Analysis

## Objective
To clean, process, and analyze Diwali sales data using Python and Excel. The goal is to explore customer demographics and product performance by leveraging pivot tables, charts, and data visualizations for meaningful business insights.

## Key Questions / KPIs

- Who are the top customers based on gender, age, and marital status?
- Which states generated the highest sales and order volumes?
- What occupations contribute most to overall purchases?
- Which product categories are most popular during Diwali?
- What is the distribution of sales across different age groups?
- Are there any patterns in customer behavior that can inform marketing strategies?

## Data Used
<a href="https://github.com/Aryan-Ag02/Python-data-analyst-project/blob/main/Diwali%20Sales%20Data.csv">Excel Analysis </a>

## Process

1. **Imported Libraries**  
   Loaded essential Python libraries like Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn for data handling and visualization.

2. **Loaded the Dataset**  
   Imported the Diwali sales CSV file and checked its structure using `.head()`, `.info()`, and `.shape()`.

3. **Data Cleaning**  
   Removed null values, dropped unnecessary columns, and handled duplicates to prepare clean data for analysis.

4. **Data Transformation**  
   Converted data types where necessary and formatted columns to enable effective grouping and analysis.

5. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**  
   Used groupby, pivoting, and visualization (bar plots, histograms) to analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.

6. **Visualization & Insights**  
   Created graphs to identify trends by gender, age, occupation, product category, and geography.

## Final Insights

- Majority of the buyers are married women aged 26–35 years.
- Most customers work in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors.
- Top-performing states in terms of sales are Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka.
- Food, Clothing, and Electronics are the most purchased product categories.
- Married individuals and working professionals show higher purchasing power during the festive season.

## Conclusion

The analysis reveals that targeting married women aged 26–35 in professional sectors with promotions on Food, Clothing, and Electronics can significantly boost Diwali sales. Businesses can use these insights to develop focused marketing campaigns and improve customer engagement in future festive seasons.
